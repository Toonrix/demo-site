Questions, Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is a good fit?

We work with integrators who have a defined build competency, clear operational capacities, and average project contract values above $40,000.

How quickly will we see results?

Most integrators see their first plant meetings land within 14–21 days of infrastructure setup. This baseline covers secure domain provisioning, server warm-up, and localized facility data validation.

How are you different from a standard lead generation agency?

Most agencies target SaaS or tech businesses using broad, generic marketing lists from software like Apollo. They don't know what a PLC or an end-of-arm tool is. We specialize exclusively in the industrial automation market, scraping specialized industrial directories and writing technical copy that addresses plant-floor constraints.

What is your guarantee?

We operate on a Performance-Linked Partnership. If we do not generate the agreed-upon quantity of qualified scoping meetings or valid RFQs in a billing cycle, we work completely free of charge until the quota is met. We tie our financial success directly to your pipeline growth.

Is this compliant with corporate IT standards?

Yes. We operate within all CAN-SPAM and corporate data regulations. Furthermore, by utilizing isolated, dedicated cold-sending domains, your primary corporate email server and IT infrastructure are 100% insulated from our outbound activities.
